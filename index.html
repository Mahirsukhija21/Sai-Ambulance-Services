Our Story

Born from a Mission to Save Lives

Sai Ambulance Services was founded with one simple but powerful belief: no one should suffer because help arrived too late. What started as a small fleet with a big heart has grown into one of India's trusted emergency medical service providers — with ambulances operating across multiple states, serving thousands of patients every year.

Our name is our commitment. Named after the timeless spirit of selfless service, Sai Ambulance brings together skilled medical professionals, modern technology, and genuine human care to deliver help when it matters most.

"Our mission is simple — to reach you in time, care for you along the way, and deliver you safely to the care you need."

ACLS — Advanced Cardiac Life Support

ACLS Ambulance

"Hospital-grade cardiac care, delivered on the move."

The ACLS Ambulance is our most advanced pre-hospital unit, specifically designed for patients experiencing life-threatening cardiac and respiratory emergencies. Equipped with the full range of cardiac intervention tools, this ambulance allows our ACLS-certified paramedics to initiate critical treatment — including defibrillation, advanced airway management, and IV drug therapy — from the moment they reach the patient.

BCLS Ambulance

ICU — Intensive Care Unit on Wheels

ICU Ambulance

"A fully functioning ICU, moving with your loved one."

The ICU Ambulance is our most intensive care vehicle — a mobile intensive care unit that replicates the capabilities of a hospital ICU in a moving environment. Designed for critically ill patients who cannot safely travel without continuous monitoring and life-support systems, this unit is essential for inter-hospital transfers of ventilated patients, post-operative critical cases, and those requiring continuous IV therapy or haemodynamic monitoring.

Our Services

The Right Care,
Every Time You Need It

From sudden emergencies to planned medical transfers, Sai Ambulance provides a full range of services to match every situation.

Emergency Ambulance 24/7

Our frontline emergency service responds to life-threatening situations — accidents, cardiac events, strokes, respiratory distress, and more. Fully equipped with advanced life support systems, our emergency ambulances are staffed by trained paramedics who begin critical care the moment they arrive at your door. We work closely with receiving hospitals to ensure seamless handover and continuity of care.

ICU Ambulance (Advanced Life Support) Critical Care

For patients who require intensive monitoring and care during transport, our ICU ambulances are equipped with ventilators, cardiac monitors, defibrillators, infusion pumps, and all essential critical care equipment. Staffed by trained ICU paramedics, these units bring hospital-level care on the road — essential for post-operative patients, trauma victims, and those in critical condition.

Inter-Hospital Patient Transfer

When patients need to move between hospitals — for specialist treatment, better facilities, or closer to family — we ensure the journey is safe, smooth, and medically supported throughout. Our transfer team coordinates with both the referring and receiving hospitals to maintain care continuity.

Neonatal & Paediatric Ambulance Specialist

Our neonatal ambulances are specially designed for the safe transport of newborns and young children. Equipped with transport incubators, neonatal ventilators, and temperature-controlled environments, these units are staffed by paramedics experienced in paediatric emergencies.

Non-Emergency Medical Transport

Whether it's a hospital appointment, a discharge, dialysis visit, or rehabilitation session, our non-emergency transport service provides comfortable, safe, and reliable transportation for patients who need medical or mobility support on the way.

Event Medical Cover

Planning a large event, sports meet, or public gathering? Our medical teams provide on-site standby ambulance services with trained first responders — ensuring that medical help is always close at hand for your guests, athletes, or audience.

Air Ambulance Coordination

For patients requiring rapid long-distance transport or remote evacuation, we coordinate air ambulance services with trusted aviation medical partners. We manage the full journey — from ground pickup to airside transfer and onward hospital handover.
